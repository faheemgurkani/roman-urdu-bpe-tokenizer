1. Bohot thakk gaya hoon.
2. Kaam khatam honay ka naam hi nahi lae rahay.
3. Apna kaam karnay ko betha too phir ghar waloon nae doosaray kaam par laga diya.
4. Mehmaan anne walay thay iss liyay sab kuch theek karna tha.
5. Safai karni thi aur kuch saamaan lana tha.
6. Kher abb kiya to kuch nahi jaa sakta.
7. Waqt to zaya ho gaya.
8. Abb kal miltay hain.
9. Allah Hafiz
